# Pickled Eggs

## Attempt One (2020-11-03)

Inspired by: [Spend with Pennies](https://www.spendwithpennies.com/pickled-eggs/)

## Ingredients

* 6 Eggs (L)
* 2 Cloves of garlic
* 250ml vinegar
* 100ml water
* 1 Tsp salt
* 1 bay leaf
* 1 habenero
* 4 Tsp mustard seed
* 1 onion

### Notes

* Kept them for four days, outside got a little gummy
* Try three next time
